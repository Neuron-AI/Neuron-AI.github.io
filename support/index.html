We are not an organization that currently works for sponsors or for money, but we do ask that, if you feel generous enough, to look at the below.

Plus, for coders, feel free to star any of our repositories on GitHub.

Climate Change is a big problem: for all of us. Yet, against its enormity and threat of imminent disaster, we do still have things that we can do to play our part.

How about changing search engine! Try Ecosia, a free chrome extension that plants trees and removes CO₂ from the atmosphere at the same time!

How about OceanHero, another free chrome extension that allows you to remove bottles from the ocean with every new tab. What's more, you can also use Ecosia as your search engine inside it!

Feeling even more generous? Perhaps donate to a charity like the World Wildlife Fund. They are international leaders in their fields and can help us all to create a world where humanity and nature can thrive.

Thank you if you have even got this far down! I hope you have helped us in some way. We hope that our technologies can be used as charityware, to encourage a small donation to charities.
